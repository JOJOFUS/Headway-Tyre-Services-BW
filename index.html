📏 How to See Your Tyre Size

Every tyre has a code printed on the sidewall — for example 205/55 R16 91 V. Understanding these numbers helps you choose the perfect fit for your vehicle.

⚡ EXAMPLE: 205/55 R16 91 V
🔹 Width (205) Tyre width in millimetres 🔹 Profile (55) Sidewall height as a percentage 🔹 Rim (R16) Radial construction & wheel diameter 🔹 Load (91) Maximum weight capacity 🔹 Speed (V) Maximum safe speed
💡 Quick tip: Check your driver's door jamb or call us for help!
