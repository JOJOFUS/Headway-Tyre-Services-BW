📋 CSV Format Accepted:
Your CSV can have these column formats:
• Item Description, Size, Qty 1, Qty 4 (recommended - matches your file)
• brand, description, size, qty1, qty4

How it works:
• Extracts brand from the beginning of Item Description (SAILUN, BLACKHAWK, etc.)
• Updates existing tyres by brand + size matching
• Updates qty1 and qty4 with your CSV values
• Skips non-tyre items (batteries, patches, services, etc.)
• Zero quantities (0) will be saved
